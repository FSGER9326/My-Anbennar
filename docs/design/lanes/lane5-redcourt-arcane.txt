# gate (verne_red_court_arcana_reform) ensures the player has committed to the mage state
# path before unlocking specialised mage advisors.

# is a tangible, immediate reward.

# A33_binding_the_beast (pos 4) [IMPLEMENTED]
# Requires: A33_the_wyvern_nest_initiative (Lane 5)
# Trigger: Mage estate privileges, ruler is mage or has ward, mage loyalty 60,
#          court mage 3 or A85 favors or 100 all power
# Effect: Seeds dragonwake path, fires verne.103, enables mythical cavalry (wyvern),
#         applies mage debt
# Commentary: This mission bridges Lane 5 (Trade — wyvern economy) and Lane 6 (Arcane —
# mage state). Binding wyverns requires both economic investment (Lane 5 nests) and
# arcane capability (mage estate). Completing it seeds the dragonwake path for the
# Red Court chain.

# A33_the_might_of_the_wyvern (pos 7) [IMPLEMENTED]
# Requires: A33_the_sea_nest (Lane 5)
# Trigger: Land forcelimit 150, naval forcelimit 100, 20 hussars (or 40 cavalry),
#          2 generals, 2 conquistadors, 2 admirals, 2 explorers, power projection 50+
# Effect: Fires verne.109, verne_might_of_the_wyvern (permanent), unlocks merc_heartspier_skyguard
# Commentary: The military manifestation of the Red Court/wyvern integration. Full-spectrum
# force projection — land, sea, and air (wyvern cavalry). The permanent modifier and
# unique mercenary company represent Verne's mage-enhanced military at its peak.
