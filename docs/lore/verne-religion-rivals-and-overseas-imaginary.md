## Overseas fantasy guidance

Derived from mission naming/design direction:

- Overseas play should read as curated expeditionary statecraft, not random colonization.
- Preferred motifs: regatta, horizon, route networks, chapterhouses, distant courts, artifact circuits.
- Blend "court spectacle" with "logistical mastery" in prose.

## Military order and mercenary tone support

For upcoming implementation around orders/merc systems:

- Present order companies as institutional arms of Verne's court doctrine.
- Use house/commandery vocabulary rather than generic sell-sword vocabulary when tied to core identity.
- Keep wyvern-associated units framed as elite state assets.
